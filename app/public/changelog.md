# Changelog

All notable changes to PunIntended will be documented in this file.

## [1.4.0] - 2026-03-29

### Added
- **Ctrl+Enter to Submit:** Keyboard shortcut to submit a pun without reaching for the mouse.
- **Phonetic Wordplay Requirement:** Puns must demonstrate genuine phonetic wordplay to score above 6 — pure wit alone won't cut it.

### Fixed
- Timestamps parsed as UTC to prevent day-boundary display errors across timezones.

## [1.3.0] - 2026-03-28

### Added
- **Auto-Refresh on Day Rollover:** Stale challenges automatically refresh when a new day begins mid-session.
- **Session Challenge History:** Track and browse the challenges from past sessions.
- **Footer Rebrand:** Footer now credits Cotlone Studios; version auto-injected from `package.json`.

## [1.2.0] - 2026-03-28

### Added
- **Speed Score:** A timer starts when you see the challenge. Faster submissions earn a speed bonus — quick wit + great pun = best outcome.

## [1.1.0] - 2026-03-28

### Added
- **AI-Powered Pun Scoring:** Puns are scored by a jaded AI comedy critic who rates creativity, humor, and groan factor.
- **Multiplayer Sessions:** Create or join sessions, invite friends via QR code, and compete head-to-head.
- **Fair Play Enforcement:** Turn-based pun submission ensures everyone gets a fair shot before you can go again.
- **Comments & Chat:** React to puns, leave comments, and chat with other players in real-time.
- **Live Notifications:** Real-time alerts when someone reacts to your pun or the host refreshes a challenge.
- **Daily Challenges:** AI-generated topic + focus combos that force creative connections between unrelated concepts.

## [1.0.0] - 2026-03-25

### Added
- **Initial Release:** Core pun game with Google sign-in, session management, and dark mode.
